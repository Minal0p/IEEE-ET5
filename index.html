Welcome to IEEE ET5 Student Branch

A student community under the patronage of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) International, we strive to empower the next generation of engineers.

About the Branch

IEEE ET5 SB is a student branch affiliated with the global IEEE institute, aiming to support and develop the technical and professional skills of students through workshops, lectures, and interactive events. We believe in building a strong community that exchanges knowledge and experiences.

Our Vision

To be leaders in empowering students with the knowledge and skills necessary for innovation in engineering and technology fields.

Our Mission

Providing a stimulating learning environment, organizing high-quality events, and building a strong professional network for our students.

Our Values

Innovation, collaboration, excellence, and social responsibility are at the core of everything we do.
